About me...

Hello, I am currently a Postgraduate Research Student at Heriot-Watt University supervised by Prof. Lynne Baillie and Dr. Wei Pang, focusing on Explainable AI, AI fairness, and Human-Computer Interaction. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from The Sichuan University of Science and Engineering in 2018. And in 2019, I got a Master's degree of Artificial Intelligence from the University of Aberdeen, with distinction. Following my graduation, I gained valuable industry experience, served in a game development company and a software company's research lab for two and a half years. After that, I received a full scholarship and start to pursue my Ph.D.
